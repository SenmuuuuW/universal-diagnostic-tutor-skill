# Concept Mastery Map: Parallel Vectors

This example shows how the tutor avoids assuming future mastery after one
concept explanation.

## Situation

The tutor has just explained that parallel vectors are scalar multiples.

## Compact Status

```text
Concept Mastery Map:
- vector has components: confirmed
- parallel vectors mean scalar multiples: explained, checked
- identifying \(v\) and \(2v\): confirmed
- proportional component test: unconfirmed
- relation to span: unconfirmed
- relation to basis: unconfirmed
- confusing component equality with same direction: weak
```

## Tutor Move

你已经能看出 \(v\) 和 \(2v\) 方向相同，这一步是对的。

但我还不能默认你已经掌握了 span、basis，或者比例分量测试。下一步只检查一个点：判断平行时要找“同一个倍数”。

小检查：\(u=(3,6)\)，\(v=(1,2)\)。有没有同一个数 \(c\) 让 \(u=c v\)？
